## Buyer State Captured

**In active operational pain.** Two distinct personas converge here:

1. **The Multi-Unit Principal** (HOA/PM/RE) — dealing with vendor compliance lapses, missed signals, system fragmentation, headcount that isn't solving the problem.
2. **The Home Services Operator** (concrete, plumbing, landscaping, HVAC, roofing, painting, GC) — scaling past 3–5 trucks, owner is the bottleneck, struggling with quote-to-cash, missed calls, and HOA preferred-vendor positioning.

Both personas share the structural problem of running portfolio operations. Both are shopping for tactical, vertical-specific solutions. Articles in this silo speak to whichever persona the title and keyword indicate.

## Pillar Page

**`/products`** — the existing Products page. Articles in this silo link upward to this page with varied anchor text, and often link to individual product pages (`/products/propertydocz`, `/products/propertyjobz`).

## Voice Register

**Tactical, specific, peer-to-peer — like a fellow principal who's been there.** Real numbers. Real workflows. Industry-native vocabulary per article (HOA, PM, real estate, restaurant — use the right terms per vertical). Practical, not philosophical.

## Internal Linking Expectations

- **Upward anchors (to `/products`):** Vary between "our product family," "vertical-specific deployments," and brand-attribute anchors. Articles tied to a specific product also link to the relevant product page directly.
- **Lateral patterns:** Articles cluster naturally by vertical. HOA-focused articles link to each other (#2, #3, #6 of MULTI-UNIT-OPS). Real estate articles cluster. Restaurant articles cluster.
- **Cross-silo:** When the article touches a "why does this matter" theme, link to the relevant `PRINCIPAL-LIFE` article via the manifesto pillar — never directly.

## Visual Treatment

**Default thumbnail type: Type A (real-world photo + data overlay).** Industry-specific scene desaturated with vibrant brand-color data overlay (signal cards, KPI tickers, briefing fragments). Type B (pure infographic) used for cross-vertical pieces.

## Health Metrics

- **Topical authority target:** Rank top-10 for "vendor compliance HOA" and "multi-unit operations" within 6 months
- **High-intent commercial conversion:** "HOA Document Revenue" article must drive booking inquiries within 30 days of publish
- **Vertical balance:** Quarterly check — articles distributed across HOA / PM / real estate / restaurant, no single vertical >60% of silo traffic
