## Internal Linking Expectations

- **Upward anchors (to `/products`):** Vary between "our product family," "vertical-specific deployments," and brand-attribute anchors. Articles tied to a specific product also link to the relevant product page directly.
- **Lateral patterns:** Articles cluster naturally by vertical. HOA-focused articles link to each other (#2, #3, #6 of MULTI-UNIT-OPS). Real estate articles cluster. Restaurant articles cluster.
- **Cross-silo:** When the article touches a "why does this matter" theme, link to the relevant `PRINCIPAL-LIFE` article via the manifesto pillar — never directly.
