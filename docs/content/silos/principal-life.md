## Buyer State Captured

**Reflective.** The Multi-Unit Principal is asking deeper questions about what success has cost them. Why am I building this? Is the way I'm building it sustainable? What was this all for?

This silo is the **brand-differentiating silo** — almost no agency or SaaS company publishes content here. Owning this territory is unique competitive moat.

## Pillar Page

**`/manifesto`** — the existing Manifesto page. Articles in this silo link upward with varied anchor text. This pillar establishes the worldview; articles in this silo apply it.

## Voice Register

**Full conviction. Founder voice. Catalyst-not-cage.** Personal, direct, declarative. Uses the chain framework (DESIRE → PRIORITIES → DECISIONS → ACTIONS → HAPPINESS) explicitly when relevant. Faith-adjacent vocabulary acceptable but never preachy. This is the silo where the BRAND_VOICE.md worldview lives most fully.

## Internal Linking Expectations

- **Upward anchors (to `/manifesto`):** Vary between "our manifesto," "the catalyst-not-cage worldview," "the Xeedly belief," and brand-attribute anchors. The manifesto is the conviction pillar that articles in this silo extend.
- **Lateral patterns:** Articles in this silo cross-reference each other heavily — the chain framework, the cage concept, and the catalyst concept recur. Use lateral links to reinforce concept cohesion.
- **Cross-silo:** Articles in `PRINCIPAL-LIFE` often pair naturally with `PRINCIPAL-INTELLIGENCE` (the "what" of intelligence platforms is the means; this silo is the "why"). When linking, route via `/manifesto` or `/platform`, not directly to articles.

## Visual Treatment

**Default thumbnail type: Type D (philosophical / conviction visual).** Abstract geometric or symbolic imagery; sparse, deliberate use of brand colors. A cage dissolving into open architecture. A chain of arrows showing the desire→happiness framework. A weighted balance scale. Type B (infographic) used for the framework piece (article #4 specifically).

## Health Metrics

- **Brand search lift:** Track quarterly increase in branded search ("Xeedly" + variants) — this silo drives brand-led traffic

- **Internal share signal:** Track manual shares (LinkedIn, email forwards) — this silo is the most shared content
